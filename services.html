What We Offer

At Magical Tales, we offer custom 3D modeling services. Whether it's a simple object or a complex structure, we can create a high-quality 3D model based on your reference images.

How It Works

  1. Submit Your Reference Images: Use our order form to upload your images and describe the 3D model you need.
  2. Get a Quote: We'll review your request and provide you with a fair quote based on the complexity of the model.
  3. 3D Modeling: Once you approve the quote, we'll start working on your 3D model.
  4. Receive Your Model: When the model is complete, we'll send you the files in your preferred format.
